Best Beef Strogonoff

Beef Stroganoff is a classic Russian dish that has become popular internationally. It typically consists of tender strips of beef, usually sirloin or tenderloin, cooked in a flavorful sauce made with sour cream, mustard, and onions. The dish is often served over egg noodles or rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package egg noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can fat-free condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder, or to taste
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Cooking Steps

  1. Saut√© ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until browned and crumbly; 5 to 10 minutes.
  2. Drain and discard any fat from the cooked beef. Stir condensed soup and garlic powder into the beef. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Remove beef from the heat. Add egg noodles and stir to combine. Stir in sour cream and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
